**Classes**

Classes define how a character approaches conflict. They shape combat roles, favored tools, and learned disciplines, but do not determine identity, origin, or allegiance. Two characters may share a class while embodying it in entirely different ways.

Each class is associated with an armor category and governs which weapons and magical disciplines can be used. Where alignment defines why a character acts, class defines how they survive the moment.

**Heavy Armor Classes**

*Built to endure pressure, dominate space, and remain standing when others would fall.*

**Crusher**

*A force that does not negotiate.*  
A relentless brute who wields blunt weapons to break bodies and morale alike. Crushers specialize in immediate, destabilizing damage, turning momentum itself into a weapon.  
Weapons: Blunt weapons

**Guardian**

*The one who does not step aside.*  
A dedicated protector trained to absorb punishment meant for others. Guardians anchor the battlefield with shield and resolve, enduring overwhelming force while keeping allies alive.  
Weapons: One-handed melee weapons, heavy shields

**Slasher**

*Violence refined to a single purpose.*  
A heavy-armored executioner focused entirely on damage output. Slashers sacrifice flexibility for raw lethality, carving through opposition with brutal efficiency.  
Weapons: Bladed weapons

**Ravager**

*Threat at the end of a chain.*  
A dangerous combatant who wields chained or flail-like weapons. The Ravager combines reach with high damage, making them a constant threat on the battlefield.  
Weapons: Chain or cord weapons

**War Mage**

*Steel guided by spellcraft.*  
A hybrid combatant who blends physical prowess with destructive and restorative magic. War Mages adapt fluidly between blade and spell, thriving where rigid roles fail.  
Weapons: All melee weapons  
Magic: Destruction, Healing

**Medium Armor Classes**

*Versatile, adaptive, and prepared for shifting conditions.*

**Hunter**

*Death from a measured distance.*  
A long-range specialist capable of using all types of bows and crossbows. The Hunter combines exceptional reach with high damage output.  
Weapons: Bows, crossbows

**Survivor**

*The one who lasts.*  
An expert in endurance and adaptation, equally at home in wastelands, towns, forests, and ruins. Survivors craft remedies, poisons, and tools to outlast both environment and enemy.  
Weapons: Short bows, blowguns

**Mercenary**

*Always fighting on their own terms.*  
A pragmatic combatant who relies on traps, preparation, and situational control. Mercenaries exploit weaknesses rather than meeting force head-on.  
Weapons: One one-handed melee weapon, one one-handed crossbow

**Duelist**

*Precision over chaos.*  
A master of controlled, one-on-one combat. Duelists specialize in repeated strikes and flawless timing, designed to dismantle powerful single opponents.  
Weapons: Rapiers, standard shields

Magic: Illusion

**Light Armor Classes**

*Fragile in form, overwhelming in influence.*

**Elementalist**

*Raw forces, shaped by will.*  
A spellcaster devoted to elemental destruction. Elementalists command fire, water, air, earth, electricity, and their volatile combinations.  
Weapons: Enchanted staff, spellbook  
Magic: Elemental destruction

**Shaman**

*Life sustained through belief.*  
A spiritual healer chosen by their peers to restore and protect. Shamans shape the battlefield with blessings, curses, and ritual focus.  
Weapons: Enchanted staff, totems  
Magic: Healing, Protection

**Conjurer**

*Never alone.*  
A summoner who fights alongside invoked entities, from minor manifestations to towering beings. Conjurers overwhelm enemies through presence rather than their own brute force.  
Weapons: Dagger  
Magic: Conjuration

**Illusionist**

*Victory through confusion.*  
A master of deception who weakens enemies through misdirection and control. Illusionists rely on distance, timing, and psychological pressure.  
Weapons: Magical scepter  
Magic: Illusion

**Runekeeper**

*Power written in advance.*  
A practitioner of rune-based magic who prepares inscriptions before battle. Runekeepers blend destructive and restorative forces through careful planning.  
Weapons: Runic stones  
Magic: Destruction, Healing
